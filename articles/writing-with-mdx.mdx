## Why this approach works

File-based content keeps the site maintainable. There is no separate admin dashboard, no schema migrations, and no hidden content state outside the repo.

## What Phase 2 adds

- frontmatter parsing
- custom MDX components
- statically generated detail pages
- one source of truth for content and presentation

That gives the portfolio a much better base for projects and writing alike.
